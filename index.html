h1>Tea

My favorite tea is Jasmine Green Tea. I find it both calming and refreshing. The floral aroma of Jasmine blossoms add a soothing, meditative quality. While the green tea leaves gives it a clean, refreshing, and slightly earthy taste. It's not too strong or bitter, making it easy to drink throughout the day. It's both comforting and energizing. Plus, known it's full of antioxidants makes it feel like a small, healthy ritual I can look forward to while taking a break or sipping soothing while I work.
